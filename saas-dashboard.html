SaaS Dashboard Concept

Concept UI/UX project combining a SaaS analytics dashboard and a marketing landing page for a subscription product. I designed navigation, card layout, data visualizations and CTA hierarchy so key metrics stay easy to scan while the landing page drives sign-ups. This case shows my approach to structuring SaaS products end-to-end, from daily-use dashboards to conversion-focused marketing surfaces.
